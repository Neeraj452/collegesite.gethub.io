About Maa Durga Ji Senior Secondary Vidyalaya

Maa Durgaji Senior Secondary Vidyalaya was established in 1980 by Late Dr. Chhavinath Singh, Reader in Applied Chemistry B.H.U. Varanasi. He was an eminent Educationist and always stood for the cause of society. He dedicated his entire life to the educational upliftment of children of remote rural areas of Jaunpur. After his sad demise his son Shri S. P. Singh the present Manager, Reader in the faculty of Law T. D. College Jaunpur, took over the responsibility with the Conviction to execute the ideas and ideals of the founder Manager Dr. Chhavinath Singh. With unflinching determination he got it affiliated to CBSE on 14th January 1994, Since then it has made a long stride in the field of Education. It is blessed with able and capable leadership, who will inspiring the generation to realise that hard work in right direction results in success.

Schoole campus

It is situated at Siddiquepur beside G.T. Road. It is in between city and V.B.S. Purvanchal university. It is 5 kms away from Jaunpur Railway Station. Situated in the lap of Nature, it is free from Hustle and Bustle of city life.>

Facilities of School

Holistic blend of cultural and co-curricular Activities. Smart Classes and internet connectivity. House Activities, Debate, Dance, Drama, Music, Craft, Painting and Public speaking, Game & Sports.
